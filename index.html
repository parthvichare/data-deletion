Data Deletion Instructions

Your privacy is important to us. If you wish to delete your data from our app, please follow the instructions below:

How to Request Data Deletion

  1. Log in to your account in our app.
  2. Navigate to the settings or privacy section.
  3. Click on the "Delete Account" or "Request Data Deletion" option.
  4. Follow the prompts to confirm your request.

Alternative Method

If you prefer, you can also send an email to support@yourwebsite.com with your request for data deletion.

Confirmation of Deletion

Once your data has been deleted, you will receive a confirmation email from us.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this process, feel free to contact us at support@yourwebsite.com.
